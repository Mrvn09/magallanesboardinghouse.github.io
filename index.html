About Us

Welcome to our boarding house, proudly owned and managed by Ruby and Mario Magallanes, both esteemed residents of Bago City. Situated in the peaceful and accessible San Gabriel Subdivision, Barangay Balingasag, at Block 21, Lot 9 & 10, our boarding house offers a harmonious blend of comfort and convenience. We cater to students and workers, providing well-furnished rooms and bedspaces that are perfect for your stay. Our commitment to creating a friendly and supportive environment ensures that every guest feels right at home.
